The African Student Association is an organization that was started in 2012. This organization was started to represent our African student population on campus. The goal of this organization was to run culturally relevant events open to the public. We want to promote our culture to help educate the public and create a better, more culturally diverse community. This organization is open to everybody to join and participate in.
