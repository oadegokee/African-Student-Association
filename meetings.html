Join us for Our Weekly Meetings

Our meetings are a chance for members of the African Student Association to come together and discuss aspects of African culture, heritage and Identity. We want to create a space where students can connect with like-minded individual, share their experiences, and learn from each other.

Our goal is to promote cultural awareness and understanding of African culture on campus. By sharing our perspectives and experiences, we hope to educate others and celebrate the rich diversity of Africam heritage. Join us and be a part of our efforts to promote cultural awareness and understanding!

Meeting Information

  • Day: Every Thursday
  • Time: 6:30pm - 7:30pm
  • Location: Multicultural Student Services Office (MSS) - CENT 1106

For any questions or concerns, wheter they are related to our meetings ot anything else, please do not hesitate to contact us. You can find our contact information on the Contact us page.
